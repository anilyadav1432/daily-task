Briards

a heart wrapped in fun

The briards, or barger de brie, is a large breed of dog traditionally used as a herder and guardian of sheep.

Breed History

The briard, ehich is believed to have originated in France,has been bred for centuries to herd and to protect sheep. The breed was used by the French Army as sentries, messangers and to search for wounded soldiers because of its fine sense of hearing,Briards were used in the First World War almost to the point of extinction.Currently the popullation of briards is slowly recovering.Charlemagne,Napoleon.Thomas Jefferson and Laffayette all owned Briards.
